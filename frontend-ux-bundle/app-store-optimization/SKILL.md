# App Store Optimization

## Overview
Maximize app visibility and download conversion rates through strategic App Store Optimization. Covers keyword research, metadata writing, visual asset optimization, and review management for both Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

## When to Use
- Writing compelling app titles, subtitles, and descriptions
- Researching and selecting high-impact keywords
- Designing screenshot sequences that convert
- Optimizing app preview videos
- Managing ratings and review responses
- A/B testing store listing elements

## Key Strategies
- Keyword density and placement optimization
- Competitor keyword analysis
- Localization for international markets
- Seasonal and trend-based keyword updates
- Conversion rate optimization through visual assets
- Review sentiment analysis and response templates
